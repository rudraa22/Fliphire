Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlipHire

How does FlipHire verify candidates?

We verify candidates through multiple checks including education verification, skill assessments, background checks, and portfolio reviews. Every candidate profile goes through our verification process before being made available to companies.

What makes FlipHire different from other hiring platforms?

FlipHire is specifically designed for freshers and entry-level candidates. We focus exclusively on 0-2 years experience candidates, ensuring companies get access to motivated talent ready to grow. Our verification process and skill-based matching make hiring faster and more effective.

Can I post multiple job openings?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can post multiple job openings. Starter plan allows up to 10 postings, Professional plan offers unlimited postings, and Enterprise plans include custom limits based on your needs.

How quickly can I start hiring?

You can start browsing candidates immediately after signing up. Most companies find suitable candidates within 24-48 hours. Our AI-powered matching helps you find the right talent faster.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all new companies. No credit card required. You can explore the platform, browse candidates, and post your first job opening completely free.

What support do you provide?

We provide email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 phone support for Enterprise customers. Our support team helps with everything from account setup to finding the right candidates.
